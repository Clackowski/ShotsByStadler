Behind The Camera

My name is Annika Stadler and I am an aspiring Physician Assistant currently living in Trumbull, Connecticut. In 2022 I became very interested in photography and bought my first professional camera in March 2023. I have developed strong skills in being able to capture beautiful images of people, landscapes and more. I am able to capture solo headshots for professional use, group images to send out as holiday cards as well as snapping photos of pets to hang on your wall. Feel free to look at my portfolio or Instagram (@shots.by.stadler) to see some of my previous work!
